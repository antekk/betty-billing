 2       AGE  AGE - (Implicit) - Identifies specific services which are payable at
 2            different rates depending upon the age of the patient.
 0
 0            G2     OVER 2 YEARS - (Implicit) - The patient is 2 years of age or older.
 0            G65    OVER 65 YEARS - (Implicit) - The patient is 65 years of age or older.
 0            G75    OVER 75 YEARS - (Implicit) - The patient is 75 years of age or older
 0            G75GP  OVER 75 YEARS- (Implicit) - The patient is 75 years of age or older. This
 0                   modifier allows physicians with the skill of GP to be paid for HSC 03.03A
 0                   or 03.03AZ at 20% above the GP rate.
 0            L1     UNDER 1 YEAR - (Implicit) - The patient is 12 months of age or younger.
 0            L10    UNDER 10 YEARS - (Implicit) - The patient has not reached their 10th
 0                   birthday.
 0            L11    UNDER 11 YEARS - (Implicit) - The patient has not reached their 11th
 0                   birthday.
 0            L13    UNDER 13 YEARS - (Implicit) - The patient has not reached their 13th
 0                   birthday.
 0            L30    UNDER 30 DAYS,ROLE OF SURGEON - (Implicit) - The patient is 30 days of age
 0                   or younger. Applies only to cardiovascular and thoracic surgery.
 0            L4     UNDER 4 YEARS - (Implicit) - The patient has not reached their 4th birthday.

 2       ANEU ANESTHETIC LISTED RATE UNITS - (Implicit) - This modifier is system derived

 2            Service Code when the anesthetic role modifier (ANE) is used.
 0
 0            ANEU   ANESTHETIC LISTED RATE UNITS - (Implicit)

 2       ANU  ANESTHETIC TIME UNITS - (Implicit) - This modifier is system derived from
 2            the information entered in the calls field when the Anesthetic Time role
 2            modifier (ANEST) is used.  EACH UNIT REPRESENTS 5 MINUTES. ADDITIONAL 5
 2            MINUTE UNITS MAY NOT BE CLAIMED UNLESS A FULL 5 MINUTES HAS ELAPSED.
 0
 0            ANU    ANESTHETIC TIME UNITS - (Implicit) - For anesthetic claimed on a time
 0                   basis, claim the entire elapsed time against the primary procedure code,
 0                   even if multiple procedures are done.  (Example:  if procedure A and B take
 0                   a combined total of 2 hours, claim 2 hours against procedure A only).  2
 0                   hours equals 24 calls.

 2       BMI  BMI - (Explicit) - This modifier is used to support the additional
 2            payment of 25% for selected procedures, obstetrical services, anesthesia,
 2            second qualified surgeon and surgical assistant services for adult patients
 2            who meet requirements indicated in the Governing Rules and patients under 18
 2            years of age who are above the 97th percentile for BMI on an approved
 2            pediatric growth curve.
 0
 0            BMIABD BMIABD - Body Mass Index, ANESTHETIC BY DEFINITION - (Explicit) -
 0                   The physician functions as the Anesthetist and is claiming a Health Service
 0                   Code (HSC) which is an anesthetic by definition and does not have modifier
 0                   ANE.
 0            BMIANE BMIANE - Body Mass Index,  ANESTHETIST - (Explicit) - The physician
 0                   functions as the Anesthetist and is claiming the listed anesthetic
 0                   benefit.
 0            BMIANT BMIANT - Body Mass Index, ANESTHETIST TRC - (Explicit) - The physician
 0                   functions as the Anesthetist and is claiming a benefit based on the
 0                   duration of the anesthetic.
 0            BMIPRO BMIPRO - Body Mass Index, SURGEON/SECOND QUALIFIED SURGEON/SURGICAL
 0                   ASSISTANT - (Explicit) - The physician functions as the Surgeon, Second
 0                   Qualified Surgeon, or as a Surgical Assistant and is claiming the listed
 0                   applicable benefit.
 0            BMISRG BMISRG - Body Mass Index, SURGEON/SECOND QUALIFIED SURGEON/SURGICAL
 0                   ASSISTANT - (Explicit) - The oral maxillofacial surgeon functions as the
 0                   Surgeon, Second Qualified Surgeon, or as a Surgical Assistant and is
 0                   claiming the listed applicable benefit. This modifier may only be claimed by
 0                   oral maxillofacial surgeons.
 0            BMI2AN BMI2AN - Body Mass Index, ANESTHETIST TRC 2 - (Explicit) - The physician
 0                   functions as the anesthetist and is claiming anesthetic time premium units
 0                   based on the duration of the anesthetic.
 0

 2            field indicates the number of services claimed.  The modifier value code
 2            indicates what each unit/call represents (time, size, number).
